Lamb Meatballs

Ingredients
  • 1 lb ground lamb
  • Greek Yogurt or sour cream
  • Kosher salt
  • Black pepper
  • Onion powder
  • Ground cumin
  • cilantro
  • Olive oil
Directions
  1. Preheat olive in a frying pan
  2. Combine ground lamb, Greek Yogurt and spices.
  3. Scoop out small portions and shape them into round meatballs
  4. Brown meatballs in olive oil, about 5 minutes
  5. Add a little bit of water, cover until cooked through, approximately 3 more minutes
